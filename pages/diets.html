Eat healthy food and live a healthy life

Healthy Fruits

Fruits provide vitamins, minerals and natural energy.

  1. Apple: Helps in digestion and keeps the heart healthy.
  2. Banana: Provides instant energy and improves stamina.
  3. Orange: Rich in Vitamin C and boosts immunity.
  4. Mango: Good for eyesight and skin health.
  • Eat seasonal fruits daily.
  • Wash fruits properly before eating.
  • Prefer whole fruits instead of juice.

Healthy Vegetables

Vegetables help improve digestion and immunity.

  1. Carrot: Improves eyesight and skin health.
  2. Tomato: Good for heart health and digestion.
  3. Spinach: Rich in iron and increases blood level.
  4. Broccoli: Boosts immunity and strengthens bones.
  • Include green vegetables daily.
  • Cook vegetables lightly to keep nutrients.
  • Wash vegetables well before cooking.

Foods & Drinks to Avoid

These foods and drinks are harmful to health.

  1. Fast Food: Increases weight and cholesterol.
  2. Soft Drinks: Contain excess sugar and harm teeth.
  3. Junk Food: Low in nutrition and weakens immunity.
  4. Excess Sugar: Can cause obesity problems.
  • Limit sugary and oily foods.
  • Avoid daily fast food consumption.
  • Drink more water instead of soft drinks.

Eating fruits and vegetables daily and avoiding junk food helps maintain a healthy body.

Healthy Exercises

Regular exercise keeps body fit and active.

  1. Walking: Improves heart health and stamina.
  2. Yoga: Keeps mind calm and body flexible.
  3. Running: Helps burn calories and stay fit.
  4. Stretching: Prevents body stiffness.
  • Exercise at least 20â€“30 minutes daily.
  • Morning exercise gives more energy.
  • Always warm up before exercise.

Eating fruits and vegetables daily, avoiding junk food, and regular exercise helps maintain a healthy body.
