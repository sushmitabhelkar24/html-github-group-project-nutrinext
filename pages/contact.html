Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

        What does a healthy diet look like?
         It emphasizes a variety of foods, specifically plenty of vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and lean          protein sources.
        What are healthy fats
         Healthy fats come from sources like avocado, nuts, seeds, and olive oil, while limiting
         saturated fats (fatty meats, butter) and avoiding trans fats.
        Why is fiber important?
         Fiber helps digestion, keeps you full, and helps manage blood sugar levels. Focus on whole          grains, vegetables, and legumes.
        How many servings of fruit and veggies do I need?
         Aim for a variety of colors, generally aiming to fill half your plate with vegetables and fruits.
        Do I need to eat breakfast?
         A balanced breakfast is generally recommended to start the day with high energy and prevent          overeating later.
